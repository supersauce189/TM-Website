Coaches

Raymond Townsend

Raymond Townsend is a trailblazing basketball coach known for his historic playing career and dedication to mentoring young athletes. As a standout at UCLA, he was part of the 1975 NCAA Championship team under Coach John Wooden and earned First-Team All-Pac-8 honors. Townsend made history as the first Filipino-American drafted into the NBA, selected by the Golden State Warriors in 1978. His professional career included playing for the Indiana Pacers and overseas in Italy and Brazil. Townsend remains a devoted mentor, guiding youth athletes through the RT Basketball Development League and the Filipino Hoops and Heritage Tour.‚Äã

Dashawn Freeman

Dashawn Freeman is a dedicated basketball coach with a distinguished playing career and commitment to developing young athletes. As a standout player at Sacramento State University during the Division 1 era, he set school records in assists and steals while becoming the Big Sky All-Time Steals Leader. Freeman earned recognition as a 2-Time All-Conference Big Sky selection and the Winningest Player in School History. In 2010, he was inducted into the Sacramento State Hall of Fame. His professional career includes pre-draft workouts with the Sacramento Kings and playing in the CBA. Now, Freeman mentors young athletes with passion and expertise.
